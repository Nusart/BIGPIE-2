Dr Anish Behl
(Endocrinologist, MBBS, MD, DM)

He suggested that we focus on Type 1 Diabetes and Type 2 Diabetes patients with 8 to 10 years of diabetes history. He also told us that most Insulin injections release the insulin into the subcutaneous tissue and the insulin gets diffused. But in healthy patients, there is insulin movement across the portal vein. So our system is possibly superior to injections as it will also be producing insulin in the gut and directly entering the blood which is similar to the natural functioning of the pancreas.
